Size

Planet size dictates the size and resolution of the simulation. Larger plant and animal populations and more complex continental shapes are possible with a bigger map.

Tilt

Planet tilt dictates which hemisphere recieves the most sun. For instance the Earth's 23.5 degree tilt means that the sourthern hemisphere recieves the most sunlight annually. Sunlight allows for dense foliage and heavy rainfall.

Rotation Direction

Planet rotation dictates which direction the rainfall flows. For instance, Earth's west to east rotation means that its greater wind currents flow east to west. Continental geography in the East cuts off warm currents from reaching the West.

Plots per Province

The more plants share a plot the more they have to compete for shared soil nutrients.

Rules

Simulations rules allow you to customize the forces that affect the plant and animal agents within the simulation. Simple rules can produce very complex behaviour.
